We are happy to announce the launch of the first MIGRATE challenge, which will allow you to get prizes while becoming informed about migration in Europe!

The challenge will be opened from December 6, 2016 and will end after a month on January 5, 2017. Joining the challenge is very easy: you will need just to create an account and play MIGRATE!

At the end of the challenge, the top 10 players in the user ranking will be awarded with the following prizes:

  • 1st classified: 50€ Amazon voucher
  • 2nd classified: 35€ Amazon voucher
  • 3rd classified: 25€ Amazon voucher
  • 4th classified: 20€ Amazon voucher
  • 5th classified: 15€ Amazon voucher
  • 6th classified: 15€ Amazon voucher
  • 7th classified: 10€ Amazon voucher
  • 8th classified: 10€ Amazon voucher
  • 9th classified: 10€ Amazon voucher
  • 10th classified: 10€ Amazon voucher

How to become one of the top players? Your total score, which is also accessible from your user profile, will depend both on the number of times you play the game and on the score of each game. The score of each game depends on the answers given to the 6 questions. More in detail, the score for each question depends on the time taken to answer the question and the weight of the question (more difficult questions give a higher score than easier ones).

As the registration to MIGRATE is totally anonymous, at the end of the challenge we will provide instructions to the top 10 players explaining how to contact us to receive the award. Amazon vouchers will be sent by the February 15, 2017. After analyzing the data from the games played by each user, the project responsible reserves the right not to award the prize to the user in case of actions considered to be unfair or malicious.
